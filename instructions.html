GAME INSTRUCTIONS

1. The game is played in turns: two human players and one A.I. Player.

2. There are 3 rounds in total. Each round will show a country's flag, and you need to complete the country's name by filling in the blanks.

3. Scoring: Players earn 1 point for each correct answer. The player with the highest score at the end wins.

4. You must fill in all blanks to submit your answer.

5. You can hover over the country's flag to reveal a hint with the continent and a fun fact about the country.

6. Player 1 and Player 2 must fill in all blanks before submitting their answer.

7. You can also scan your QR code for your answer's country and move ahead.

8. After filling in all blanks, click the Submit button to submit your answer or press enter key.

9. The computer will try to guess the country's name based on its stored answer.

10. At the end, you will see the final scores for Player 1, Player 2, and the A.I. Player.
